Minecraft Mod: Colores

The Minecraft Mod, Colores... Colour... Ores... Colores? Geddit? No? Okay, on with the post.

Colores aims to make obtaining dyes within Minecraft easier by generating ores throughout the world that can be transformed into their respective dyes.

    Colores Guide

    Okay, first things first, find a colore. They look like the picture below.



    Dig those things up and get "Dye Fossils". They look like this.



    Next, you can either make a "Portable Dye Extractor" or a "Dye Extractor". First we'll talk about the "Portable Dye Extractor. This is how you craft it.



    Next, go ahead and take your "Dye Fossil" and place it in a crafting grid with a water bottle and violà! You have the dye that the fossil relates to!
    Be careful though! The "Portable Dye Extractor" has a limited number of uses! Side Note: The recipes are shapeless.



    Next onto the "Dye Extractor". This is a stationary block that will extract dyes from fossils without breaking.
    First, craft a "Dye Extraction Kit", recipe below.



    Now you can craft a "Dye Extractor". Recipe below.



    Place it down, right click it and BAM! A whole new GUI.
    But Chris, "What do do all the slots do?" I hear you ask. Well, look below and you'll see.



    Dye in the left slot, fuel in the bottom. Fuel is either a Water Bucket (You get the bucket back), or a Water Bottle (You dont get the bottle back).
    The Water Bucket also lasts twice as long.



    Thats about it! Thanks for checking out the mod, hope you have fun with it!
